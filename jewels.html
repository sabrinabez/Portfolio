Disney Jewels Branding for Walmart

Walmart wanted to launch a new line of higher end Disney jewelry for their stores, with price tags ranging between $60 - $300 dollars. I was tasked with designing a logo and look for the brand, knowing just the price range, the trends, and the name "jewels". I concepted some geometric approaches, which I was told to skew towards, and came up with a variety of results. The end result is beautiful yet playful, being youthful yet refined, fit for all ages. It was a pleasure to work on the project, and both Disney and Walmart was extremely happy with the brand look. You can now find Disney Jewels at Walmarts nationwide.
