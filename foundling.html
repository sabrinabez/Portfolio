Foundling

Movie Title Sequence

Movie title sequence was made based on the book "Foundling" by D.M Cornish, which is an industrial fantasy book about a young boy named Rosamund who goes on a journey through a land of monsters with the help a female monster hunter named Europe.

To keep true to the essence of the high-fantasy trilogy, the illustrations and maps of the original author were used in the video.
