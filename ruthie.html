Ruthie Davis X Disney Cobrands

Specialty Branding & Packaging

Ruthie Davis Cobranding for Frozen 2

I got to work directly with Ruthie Davis to design the box for her cobrand with Frozen 2. I designed a variety of styles that fit the Frozen 2 brand as well as her own fierce style. The end result is a frozen twist on Ruthie's traditional black shoe box. She loved the idea of the four elements being in the corners of the box, which became very ownable to her cobrand since we did not do that with any other Frozen 2 cobrands.

Ruthie Davis X Disney Princess Jasmin Romance Card

Ruthie wanted a special romance card for her Princess Jasmin shoe line. The box would follow the standard Ruthie X Disney Princess rule, which allowed us to have more freedom and creativity with the card. The card needed to invoke feelings of the shoe line while also feeling unique to Jasmin. I had an idea to use Jasmin's tiger Rajah on the card, and both the brand team and Ruthie Davis loved it.
