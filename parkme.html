parkMe Smart Parking

Ever experienced the frustration of driving around a parking lot, unable to find a spot? With parkMe, the experience is made easy by allowing the user to reserve and pay for parking spots of their choice as well as view available parking spots in the surrounding area. Product concept produced by Sabrina Bezar and Kayleigh Rago.

Persona: Emma

Emma is a 25-year-old woman who likes to go shopping on the weekends. She often finds herself struggling to find parking spots at busy malls and other event locations. She doesn't like spending that much time looking for spots, and she rarely carries enough cash for the more pricey venues. Emma drives a 2015 Toyota Corolla and doesn't like fighting for spots in tight parking lots.

Choose a lot

Before you head out, open the ParkMe app and input your destination. The app will display all of the parking lots near your location.

Comfirm Location

ParkMe app will display the number of spots available on each floor along with the hourly rate.

Pick a floor

After selecting a floor, the app will display the lot name and address to help you find your way. The hourly rate will be displayed so you know before you get there. Once you arrive at the lot, press the check in button to open the gate. This button also starts your time at the lot and will keep track of how long youâ€™ve been there.

Check in & out

While you are out, the app keeps track of how long you have been parked at your lot. Pay through the app using a credit card or paypal.
