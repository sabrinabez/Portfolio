Brighton Template Library

The Brighton Template Library was an online library of key template examples used in different content areas of Brighton.com. The template library allowed for quick access to multiple layouts, making it faster and easier to compare formats and pull code for future pages and updates.

My Role

Both websites showcased on this page were entirely wireframes, designed, and coded by myself using Adobe XD and Adobe Dreamweaver, keeping in mind Brighton's brand standards for the web. They were active on the site from 2017-2022.

Website was built for internal use only and showcased over 33 templates.

Why Brighton? Webpages

Collaborated with HR at Brighton to create a unique set of webpages utilizing the Template Library. HR provided testimonials of employees at Brighton, objective was to create a friendly and engaging website for potential hires to view.
