All About Sabrina

I'm a UX/UI designer from the heart of Orange County, California. I am currently based in San Diego, where I have lived since 2021. My specialties are UX/UI Product Design, Branding, and Packaging. I believe a designer should know the code behind what they're designing, and how to effecively communicate with developers and work around back-end limitations. That's why I have a minor in Computer Science. I am familiar with several coding languages, and coded this website myself!

I am a graduate of California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. I've got a passion for solving problems, getting things done, and doing what's right. I have enough energy for a power plant and I'm an extremely fast learner. I'm super flexible and love doing new things, so I've got quite a few tools on my belt already!
