Pixar Branding and Packaging for Mattel

Early 2019 it was time for Pixar to get a rebrand. The old branding had been used for years and the blue had become iconic to Pixar, but it needed an update. My department was tasked with the rebrand after initial concepts from the Pixar team, and when my designs were selected I was put on task to flesh out the entire rebrand for both the Mattel line and for Mass Market.

My Role

I reported to the Pixar team on this project from initial blue sky concepts to nearing the final dielines. I took a high attention to detail with color, composition, typography, and even the shape of the dielines themselves. The designs I presented to Pixar were through many iterations, and the final design pleased both the Pixar and Mattel teams.

When my contract finished at Disney, they were still deciding between Purple or the Iconic blue. I'm happy to see they went with the iconic blue after all.

Pixar Color Story

The Pixar team wanted all the details. I picked pantones for each character in their roster and fleshed out color stories for every story they tell. The results are seen in the packaging for Mattel.
