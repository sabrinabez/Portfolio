All About Me

I'm a UX/UI designer from the heart of Orange County, California. My specialties are User Experience, User Interface, Branding, and Packaging. I believe a designer should know the code behind what they're designing, and how difficult things can be for the developers. That's why I have a minor in User Experience, and have learned several coding languages including HTML, CSS, Javascript, Java, and Python. I even coded this website myself!

I am a graduate of California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. I've got a passion for solving problems, getting things done, and doing what's right. I have enough energy for a power plant and I'm an extremely fast learner. I'm super flexible and love doing new things, so I've got quite a few tools on my belt already.

Hobbies
I am an avid gamer and I dream of doing work on my favorite games. My current favorite games are Overwatch and Hearthstone, of which I rank in Gold for Overwatch and Diamond 5 for Hearthstone.
