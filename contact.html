Hello!

I'm a UX/UI designer from the heart of Orange County, California. My specialties are User Experience, User Interface, Branding, and Packaging. I believe a designer should know the code behind what they're designing, and how difficult things can be for the developers. That's why I have a minor in User Experience, and have learned several coding languages including HTML, CSS, Javascript, Java, and Python. I even coded this website myself!

I am recent a graduate of California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. I've got a passion for solving problems, getting things done, and doing what's right. I also have an insatiable apetite for cartoons as well as sci-fi and fantasy media. I have enough energy to power a power plant and I'm an extremely fast learner!

I'm currently a Designer & Product Development Manager for MGA Entertainment. Previously, I was at Disney Consumer Products as a Design and Branding associate, and before that I was at The Walt Disney Company as a User Experience Associate. I'm super flexible and love doing new things, so I've got quite a few tools on my belt already!

If all this and more sounds good to you, I'd love to have a chat some time. The sky's the limit!
