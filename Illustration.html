Illustrations

I am a classically trained artist who is well-versed in both digital and traditional techniques for creating paintings and illustrations. I can create illustrations in a variety of styles depending on the needs of the project, and can create works in Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Procreate, Clip Studio Pro, or a combination of softwares as well as with traditional materials and mediums if needed.
